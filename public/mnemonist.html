The Rules

How to Play

  • IThe GridA 5×5 matrix of 25 cells will appear. 10 randomly chosen cells will each hold a unique object.
  • IIObservation — 20 SecondsStudy the grid carefully and memorise which object sits in which cell. The clock is ticking!
  • IIIPreparation — 15 SecondsThe grid vanishes. Use this time to mentally rehearse the positions before recall begins.
  • IVRecall — 30 SecondsThe 10 objects appear on the left. Select one, then click the cell where it belongs. You may pick up a placed item and move it to a different cell.
  • VScoringOne point for each object placed in its correct cell. Maximum score: 10 points.
  • VILeaderboardYour score is recorded. Rise through the ranks and claim your place in the Hall of Memory!
